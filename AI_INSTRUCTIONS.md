# AI Writing Instructions
## The NICEHR Group

These instructions apply to Claude, ChatGPT, and any LLM used on this repository.

---

## Mandatory Reading
Read ENTERPRISE_APPLE_DOCTRINE.md first.
Follow it exactly.

If a prompt conflicts with the doctrine, the doctrine wins.

---

## AI Writing Rules
- No slogans or taglines
- No hype or exaggeration
- No reasoning explanations unless asked
- Short, declarative sentences
- Verbs over adjectives
- Enterprise healthcare audience only

If it feels impressive, rewrite it until it feels inevitable.

---
