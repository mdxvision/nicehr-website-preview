NICEHR Bootcamp

Empowering Heroes.
Building Futures.

A transformative program training veterans and unemployed Americans for rewarding, high-impact careers in healthcare IT.

The Mission

From Service to Healthcare IT.

Veterans bring discipline, leadership, and problem-solving excellence that translate perfectly to healthcare IT. Our bootcamp program provides the technical foundation to transform those skills into a thriving career.

For unemployed Americans seeking a new path, healthcare IT offers stability, purpose, and growth. We're opening that door wide.

  • ✓ Flexible online training platform
  • ✓ Hands-on EHR sandbox experience
  • ✓ Industry-recognized skills
  • ✓ Pathway to employment

The Program

How NICEHR Bootcamp Works.

Learn

Comprehensive online training covering EHR fundamentals, workflows, and best practices. Learn at your own pace.

Practice

Get hands-on experience with real EHR systems in sandbox environments provided by Epic, Cerner, and other vendors.

Launch

Upon completion, graduates have the option to work remotely or on-site with our client healthcare organizations.
