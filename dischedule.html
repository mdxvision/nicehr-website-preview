The DiSC Framework

Four Styles. Infinite Possibilities.

DiSC is the world's leading behavioral assessment, trusted by over 1 million professionals annually.

D - Dominance

Traits: Direct, results-oriented, decisive, competitive

Best for: High-pressure go-lives, executive training, rapid problem-solving

i - Influence

Traits: Enthusiastic, optimistic, collaborative, expressive

Best for: Group training, change management, team motivation

S - Steadiness

Traits: Patient, reliable, supportive, methodical

Best for: Detailed workflow training, one-on-one support, nervous learners

C - Conscientiousness

Traits: Analytical, precise, systematic, quality-focused

Best for: Technical training, compliance, documentation, testing
